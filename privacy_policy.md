# Healing Notes Privacy Policy

This application does not capture any data and the user's input never leaves the app.  User input is solely for adding or updating a frequency or binaural beat for listening.  Consider this a guarantee that, given that this application is only downloaded and installed from the Google Play store and is never altered by the user, no data will be captured and you will have complete privacy.  I understand that installing from FDroid or in any way directly onto the phone is useful and I do not disparage those who do.  Obviously, user beware at that point.

That being said, this application has reviews activated on Google Play.  If you utilize the reviews feature, your review and rating will be collected.  This is not a feature of my app, but of the Play Store.
